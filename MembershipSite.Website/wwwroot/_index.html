Member Only Area

For pages or files that only approved members should have access to, upload them to the /secure folder. Any attempt to access them will redirect the visitor to a member login page. Once successfully logged in, they will then be redirected to the content they were trying to access.

For example, trying to access the page at /secure/index.html when not logged in will redirect you to a login page.
